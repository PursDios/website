biddingTest

biddingtest.ryancooper.co.uk

An unglamorous auction sandbox. Start a room, set rules (English, Dutch, sealed-bid), watch the bids roll in, see what the edge cases actually do at 3am when two bots disagree about the tick-size.

Not a product. A place to understand. I'd rather get the mechanics right in a toy than wrong in a real codebase.
